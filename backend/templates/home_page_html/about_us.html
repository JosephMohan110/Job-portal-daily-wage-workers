About WorkConnect

Connecting daily wage workers with opportunities, building better futures together

Our Story

How WorkConnect began and our journey so far

WorkConnect was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between daily wage workers and employment opportunities. Our founders recognized the challenges faced by both workers seeking consistent employment and employers needing reliable labor.

What started as a small local initiative has grown into a nationwide platform connecting thousands of skilled workers with employers across various industries. We've helped construction workers, agricultural laborers, domestic helpers, and many others find meaningful work while providing businesses with access to a reliable workforce.

Today, WorkConnect is proud to be a trusted name in the gig economy, facilitating connections that empower workers and provide quality service to employers. Our platform continues to evolve, incorporating new features and technologies to better serve our growing community.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us forward every day

Our Vision
